PAYZENIX LOGO SETUP INSTRUCTIONS
=================================

To add your PayZenix logo to the application:

1. Save your logo image (the phoenix design with orange/blue colors) as:
   payzenix-logo.png

2. Place it in this folder:
   payzenix-payroll-main/public/

3. The logo will automatically appear in:
   - Sidebar (top left)
   - Login page
   - All pages that use the Logo component
